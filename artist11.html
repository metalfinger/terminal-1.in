LIFAFA
Lifafa is Suryakant Sawhney, founder of Peter Cat of Recording Co, the celebrated psychedelic ensemble based out of Delhi. The live electronic music he performs as“Lifafa” presents a new and original soundscape, one far more inspired by the sounds of India itself, from both folk and religious music to vintage Bollywood. The sounds of Lifafa skirt between Disco and House music to harmonium based Bhajans and tribal sounds on top of which Suryakant sings in both Hindi and English resulting in a unique and captivating experience.
